Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

To create a new service, navigate to the Services section from the sidebar. Click on "Add New Service" button, fill in the service details including name, duration, price, and description. You can also set availability and assign colors for better organization.

Your analytics dashboard is the main page when you log in. It shows key metrics including revenue, appointment counts, service performance, and client analytics. You can export detailed reports using the export button in the top navigation.

Yes! Use the theme switcher button (palette icon) in the bottom right corner. You can switch between light and dark modes, and choose from 8 different color themes including blue, purple, green, orange, red, teal, pink, and indigo.

Notification settings can be found in your Profile section. You can configure email notifications for new appointments, cancellations, reminders, and other important events. You can also set your notification preferences for different types of activities.

First, try refreshing the page using the refresh button in the top navigation. If issues persist, check your internet connection. For ongoing problems, use the contact form below to report the issue with detailed information about what you were trying to do when the problem occurred.

You can export your data from multiple places: Use the export button on the Analytics Dashboard for comprehensive reports, or visit individual sections (Past Appointments, Services) and use their respective export functions. Data is exported in JSON format with detailed information.

Understanding Analytics

Make data-driven decisions with your dashboard insights

trending_up Key Metrics

  • Total Revenue: Your complete earnings with monthly breakdown
  • Total Appointments: All scheduled appointments including today's count
  • Active Services: Number of available vs total services
  • Success Rate: Percentage of completed vs cancelled appointments

bar_chart Charts & Graphs

  • Revenue Chart: Track financial trends over time
  • Service Performance: See which services are most popular
  • Peak Hours: Understand your busiest appointment times
  • Status Distribution: View appointment completion rates

Analytics Tips

  • insights Use revenue trends to identify your best performing months
  • schedule Check peak hours to optimize your availability
  • stars Identify VIP clients for special treatment and retention
  • download Export reports for tax purposes or business planning
