Welcome to the Acuity Scheduling Extension by Xtracut Systems!
Our extension is designed to provide you with a seamless experience for managing your appointments and scheduling. We've incorporated two dropdown menus that offer you flexibility and control over your workflow.
The first dropdown menu let's you to decide whether new person contacts should be added to Leads or Contacts.
The second dropdown menu let's you to decide whether new appointments should be added to Tasks or Meetings.
The third dropdown menu let's you to decide whether new contact phone numbers should be added as Phone or Mobile.
The fourth dropdown menu let's you to turn the Notifications On or Off.
The fourth dropdown menu let's you to decide wheather to send Private Calendars in Email.
